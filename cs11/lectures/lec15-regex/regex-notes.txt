Regular Expressions
- Used for 1. Validation, 2. Extraction, 3. Replacement

Two ways to do it in JS:

1. /pattern/flags
2. let regex = new RegExp("pattern", "flags");

regex.test("testinput") // boolean
string.match(regexpattern)
string.replace(regexpattern, newstring)

/ab+/.exec("abbb") returns the longest match "abbb", not the shortest match.

The second is useful when you don't know what the regular expression is
until runtime (e.g. taking input text)
